Why presence

I invite you to close your eyes for a moment and take a slow deep breath in and out.

Slow inhale. Slow exhale.

What did you notice? Did any feelings, sensations, or thoughts pass by? Does stress or worry feel like the loudest voice inside of you? Are you feeling pulled in one thousand directions? Do you feel like you're not living your own life?

Working with me is an invitation to slow down, listen to yourself, and free yourself from shoulds and expectations. Together we'll cultivate the inner perspective that helps you trust yourself and your choices.

My background

My life is oriented around the practice of presence. I am a longtime meditator in the Zen tradition and a practitioner of yoga, pilates, and somatic experiencing.

I am a certified Anusara alignment-based yoga instructor and have explored Reichian, Jungian, and Hakomi therapy.

I’ve also worked in technical leadership at a biotech company, helping teams navigate uncertainty while maintaining clarity and focus in decision-making.
