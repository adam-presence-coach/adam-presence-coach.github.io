Adam Drake Presence Coach

I offer structured support of your life as a presence coach. We will explore the feeling of presence together and codevelop strategies for bringing more presence into your life. No matter where you are on your journey we'll find ways to deepen your understanding of presence through concrete actions that you can begin practicing in this moment.

What we'll work on
  • We'll practice the action of letting go with ease and grace.
  • We'll look at ways to improve focus and attention.
  • We'll notice our feelings and thoughts and develop choice on what to do next.
  • We'll explore showing up as our full selves to the present moment.
