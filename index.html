Why presence

I invite you to close your eyes for a moment and take a slow deep breath in and out. What did you notice? Did any feelings, sensations, or thoughts pass by? Does stress or worry feel like the loudest voice inside of you? Are you feeling pulled in one thousand directions? Do you feel like you're not living your own life and that someone else is making all your decisions? Do you feel a sense of emptiness or an absence of meaning?

Working with me is an invitation to slow down, to listen to yourself, and to free yourself of shoulds and expectations. We'll focus on cultivating your inner perspective so that you can trust yourself and the choices you make. We'll practice listening to yourself together safely, gentle, and kind so that you can begin living your life with conviction and energy.

My skills and expertise

My life is oriented around the practice of presence. I live the practice I offer to you; it's an active daily cultivation I've centered for many years of my life. I am a long time meditator in the zen tradition and practitioner of yoga, pilates, and somatic experiencing. I am a certified Anusara alignment-based yoga instructor. I've deeply explored and practiced Reichian, Jungian, and Hakomi therapy.

I am commited to clarity, trust, and kindness while staying in relationship with the negative feelings that arise. I have a rich life experience working in kitchens, bars, a scientist, a musician, a traveler, and a dancer. I have a master's in biochemistry and I worked for nearly 10 years as the first employee of a successful biotech company. I've learned how to work with diverse groups of people to get difficult stuff done in stressful environments. I've grown expertise in maintaining focus along with communication strategies to help everyone I'm working with act in alignment.

I bring everything I've learned to working with you to bring structure to your life so that you experience and live the life you want to with more presence and intention.

What you'll practice in session

I offer structured support of your life as a presence coach. We will explore the feeling of presence together and codevelop strategies for bringing more presence into your life. No matter where you are on your journey we'll find ways to deepen your understanding of presence through concrete actions that you can begin practicing in this moment.

  • You will practice the action of letting go with ease and grace.
  • You will look at ways to improve focus and attention.
  • You will notice your feelings and thoughts and develop choice on what to do next.
  • You will explore showing up as your full self to the present moment.
